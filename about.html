About Us | CleanCoast Cape Town

Our Mission

We aim to create a sustainable, clean, and beautiful coastline for Cape Town. Every cleanup event brings us closer to a future where plastic pollution is something we read about, not walk on.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by local volunteers, CleanCoast Cape Town began with a simple idea: small actions make big waves. What started as a weekend project is now a community-wide initiative supported by schools, students, and local businesses.
